Welcome to thecuttycorner.com, a mostly outdoor-centered blog by Jake Jackson. This website began development in late 2024 with the goal of a homologous space for life's happenings. This website came from a desire to share, but a distaste with doing so through social media. After a few previous attempts of building a site with some of the popular tools available today, I never felt like the site design matched what I was going for. Modern website design is a bit boring, so I retreated to the even more boring ways of the old internet, as well as some of my favorite blog-style websites that still exist today. Shout out to bigsurtrailmap.net, goingslowly.com, buggulch.com, and the various homemade travel blogs for inspiring me to code this myself. This website serves as a refuge for my projects, trips, articles, and other creations that will grow with time. Enjoy.

This page is in the early phases of its life, so if you see something that looks awry, email thecuttycorner@gmail.com
